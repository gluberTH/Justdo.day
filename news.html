Version 1.0 is Live

We've officially launched the MVP of justdo.day. The core features are fully functional right inside your browser without any registration required.

  • Track up to 3 habits: Focused minimalism to prevent burnout.
  • The 2-Misses Rule: A realistic approach to consistency. One miss is okay, two breaks the chain.
  • One-click Sharing: Generate beautiful images to post your streak to Instagram or Twitter directly from your device.

We are currently working on a Pro version that will include user accounts, cloud sync, and cross-device support via email/Google authentication.
